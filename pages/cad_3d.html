I've been making 3D models with CAD software since I was in middle school, and over the years I've grown to really enjoy it. In 2024, I bought a 3D printer, and I've been using it almost nonstop since! I've been modeling little doodads for various purposes and printing them. Below are a few of the things I've made!

Baldwin Organ Keys

When two of the keys on my beloved 1962 Baldwin Orgasonic electric organ were broken by a falling radio, I took matters into my own hands! With the pieces of the broken keys, I used Autodesk Fusion and a caliper to create very accurate models that I was able to print and install onto my organ. The models are parameterized, so that by only changing a few numbers I can print any of the keys! I have already uploaded the finished keys to Thingiverse, but I'll host them here as well for your convenience!
